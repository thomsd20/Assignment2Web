About Us

Wall Street Wines opened in June 1860, the product of David Smith's and Jamie Dacey's idea that Dunedin might support a shop that indulged their love for naturally made wines from artisanal small producers.Street Wall Wines is a shop where everyone who works here actually loves wine, drinks wine with food, and knows at least a little bit about what we're selling.
